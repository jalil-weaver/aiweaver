# State 1 - Change Scope Definition

### **Goal**

Define and delimit the exact scope of the accepted change with no remaining ambiguity.

---

## **State Owner**

Project / Product Lead

---

## **Required Functions**

- Project / Product Lead

---

**Entry Conditions**

- State 0 closed with change accepted

**Exit Conditions**

- Change scope clearly defined and delimited

---

### **Actions**

**Action 1 – Define Functional Delta**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: describe what is added, modified, or removed by the change.

Must be true after: Change Specification drafted, explicitly listing functional deltas.

---

**Action 2 – Identify Non-Impacted Scope**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: explicitly state what remains unchanged.

Must be true after: change boundaries explicit; no ambiguity on non-impacted scope.

---

**Action 3 – Decide Design Involvement**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: decide whether design work is required for the change.

Must be true after: change explicitly marked as **No design required** or **Design update required**.

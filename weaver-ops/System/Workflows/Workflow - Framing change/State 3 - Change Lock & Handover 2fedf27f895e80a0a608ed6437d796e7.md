# State 3 - Change Lock & Handover

### **Goal**

Freeze the validated change definition and hand it over to the appropriate estimation flow.

---

## **State Owner**

Project / Product Lead

---

## **Required Functions**

- Project / Product Lead

---

**Entry Conditions**

- Technical decision completed

**Exit Conditions**

- Change locked
- Routed to estimation

---

### **Actions**

**Action 1 – Validate and Lock Change Specification**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: freeze the change definition.

Must be true after: Change Specification validated and locked; no further modification allowed without restart.

---

**Action 2 – Route to Estimation**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: send the change to the correct estimation flow.

Must be true after: change handed over to **Development Estimation** or **Development + Infrastructure Estimation**.

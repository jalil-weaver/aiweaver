# State 0 - Change Qualification & Lock

### **Goal**

Capture and qualify a change request to decide whether it enters the framing flow or is rejected or redirected.

---

## **State Owner**

Project / Product Lead

---

## **Required Functions**

- Project / Product Lead

---

**Entry Conditions**

- Change request received
- Existing product context available

**Exit Conditions**

- Change accepted and qualified
    
    **or**
    
- Change rejected or redirected

---

### **Actions**

**Action 1 – Capture Change Request**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: formally capture the requested change.

Must be true after: change request documented and traceable.

---

**Action 2 – Qualify Change Eligibility**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: decide if the change is valid to frame.

Must be true after: change explicitly marked as accepted, rejected, or redirected.

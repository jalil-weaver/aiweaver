# State 3 - Final Validation

### **Goal**

Validate the delivered product against technical and functional requirements and authorize production release.

---

## **State Owner**

Delivery Owner

---

## **Required Functions**

- Delivery Owner
- Tech Lead
- Project / Product Lead
- Client Interface (Delivery Side)

---

**Entry conditions**

- State 2 closed
- All planned features developed
- No blocking or critical bugs

**Exit conditions**

- Product validated for production
- Final payment confirmed
- Production switch authorized

---

## Actions

**Action 1 – Perform Final Technical Validation**

R: Tech Lead · A: Tech Lead

Purpose: confirm the product is technically stable and production-ready.

Must be true after: no blocking or critical issues; release candidate approved.

---

**Action 2 – Perform Functional Validation**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: confirm delivered features match validated scope and design.

Must be true after: functional acceptance confirmed; no unresolved scope deviation.

---

**Action 3 – Secure Production Go Authorization**

R: Client Interface (Delivery Side) · A: Delivery Owner

Purpose: secure formal authorization to deploy to production and confirm commercial clearance.

Must be true after: production switch explicitly authorized; final payment confirmed.

# State 2 - Estimation Consolidation

## Goal

Finalize executable delivery scenarios and make them usable for Sales without exposing internal costs.

---

## State Owner

Delivery Owner

---

## Required Functions

- Delivery Owner
- Project / Product Lead
- Client Interface (Delivery Side)
- Quality & Process Guardian

---

**Entry Conditions**

- At least one delivery scenario produced

---

**Exit Conditions**

- Estimation finalized and shared with Sales

---

### **Actions**

**Action 1 — Consolidate Scenarios 
R: Project / Product Lead · A: Delivery Owner**

Purpose: ensure internal coherence and executability.

Result: scenarios validated internally.

**Action 2 — Publish Estimation Output** 

R: Quality & Process Guardian · A: Delivery Owner

Purpose: provide Sales with sellable scenarios.

Result: Sales receives delivery scenarios **without internal costs**.

Action 3 – Publish Estimation Output to Sales

R: Client Interface (Delivery Side) · A: Delivery Owner

Purpose: provide Sales with sellable delivery scenarios.

Must be true after: Sales receives finalized delivery scenarios without internal costs or internal-only data.

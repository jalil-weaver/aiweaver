# State 2 - Design Definition

### **Goal**

Define the complete user experience and remove any ambiguity on how the product will look and behave.

---

## **State Owner**

Project / Product Lead

---

## **Required Functions**

- Project / Product Lead
- UX/UI Designer

---

### **Entry Conditions**

- State 1 closed
- Product Documentation validated and scope locked

---

### **Exit Conditions**

- Design completed
- Animated prototype completed
- Design system defined
- **Design validated as a whole**

---

## **Actions**

**Action 1 – Design User Journeys and Screens**

R: UX/UI Designer · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: translate the functional scope into concrete user interfaces.

Must be true after: all screens and user journeys designed; no missing UI states.

---

**Action 2 – Build Animated Prototype**

R: UX/UI Designer · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: make the product behavior tangible and reviewable.

Must be true after: animated prototype reflects all main flows and interactions.

---

**Action 3 – Define Design System**

R: UX/UI Designer · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: ensure visual and interaction consistency.

Must be true after: core components, styles, and interaction rules defined.

---

**Action 4 – Validate Design Against Product Scope**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: ensure design strictly matches the validated product scope.

Must be true after: no functional deviation; no missing or extra features.

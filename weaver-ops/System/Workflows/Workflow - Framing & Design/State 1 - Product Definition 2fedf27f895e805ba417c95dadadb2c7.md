# State 1 - Product Definition

### **Goal**

Define **what will be built**, exhaustively, and lock the functional scope.

---

## **State Owner**

Project / Product Lead

---

## **Required Functions**

- Project / Product Lead

---

### **Entry Conditions**

- State 0 closed
- Scope Intent validated by the client

---

### **Exit Conditions**

- [Product Documentation](../../Related%20documents/Product%20Documentation%202fddf27f895e80bba212d437b6395575.md) completed and validated
- Product scope locked
- [Product Roadmap](../../Related%20documents/Product%20Roadmap%20301df27f895e8021a83ccc7f2ead5334.md) produced

---

## **Actions**

**Action 1 – Define Features and User Flows**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: translate the locked direction into a complete functional scope.

Must be true after: all features identified; main user flows defined end-to-end.

---

**Action 2 – Write Functional Behaviors (Gherkin)**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: remove ambiguity by expressing expected behaviors precisely.

Must be true after: all key behaviors written in Gherkin; no interpretation gaps remain.

---

**Action 3 – Define Business Rules and Edge Cases**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: capture non-obvious logic and constraints.

Must be true after: business rules explicit; edge cases identified and documented.

---

**Action 4 – Validate and Lock Product Scope**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: formally freeze the functional scope.

Must be true after: Product Documentation validated; scope locked; no further changes allowed without restart.

---

**Action 5 – Build Product Roadmap**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: project the validated product scope into a multi-month roadmap to support prioritization and future delivery planning.

Must be true after: roadmap covers multiple months, reflects priorities and dependencies, and is consistent with the locked product scope.

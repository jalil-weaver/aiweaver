# State 0 - Need Clarification & Scope Intent Lock

### **Goal**

Clarify the client need, lock the project direction, and create an explicit early commitment before detailed framing starts.

---

## **State Owner**

**Project / Product Lead**

---

## **Required Functions**

- Project / Product Lead
- Delivery Owner
- Client Interface

---

**Entry Conditions**

- Commercial agreement signed
- Expression of Need available

---

### **Exit Conditions**

- Need clarified
- Direction agreed
- Success criteria defined
- Scope Intent validated by the client

---

## **Actions**

**Action 1 – Review Expression of Need**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: ensure the client need is clearly understood and complete.

Must be true after: target, pain points, objectives, and constraints are understood; no ambiguity blocking direction.

---

**Action 2 – Clarify Direction and Boundaries**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: define the intended direction and boundaries of the project.

Must be true after: high-level in-scope and out-of-scope identified; assumptions made explicit.

---

**Action 3 – Define Success Criteria**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: agree on how success will be evaluated.

Must be true after: success criteria defined, clear, and measurable enough to guide scope decisions.

---

**Action 4 – Draft Scope Intent**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: formalize the agreed direction into a single reference document.

Must be true after: Scope Intent drafted, reflecting direction, boundaries, assumptions, and success criteria.

---

**Action 5 – Validate Scope Intent with Client**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: secure explicit client validation before proceeding.

Must be true after: Scope Intent validated by the client; explicit decision lock achieved.

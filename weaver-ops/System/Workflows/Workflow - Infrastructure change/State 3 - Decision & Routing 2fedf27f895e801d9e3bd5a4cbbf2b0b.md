# State 3 - Decision & Routing

## Goal

Make a clear decision on the infrastructure change proposal and route the outcome.

---

## State Owner

Delivery Owner

---

## Required Functions

- Delivery Owner
- Tech Lead

---

## Entry Conditions

- Infrastructure Change Proposal completed

---

### **Exit Conditions**

- Proposal approved, rejected, or sent for adjustment

### **Actions**

**Action 1 – Review Infrastructure Change Proposal** 

R: Tech Lead · A: Tech Lead

Purpose: validate technical coherence and feasibility.

Must be true after: proposal technically approved or rejected.

**Action 2 – Route Outcome** 

R: Delivery Owner · A: Delivery Owner

Purpose: decide next steps based on the decision.

Must be true after:

- Approved → execution or sale triggered
- Rejected → no infra work allowed
- Needs adjustment → proposal updated and re-reviewed

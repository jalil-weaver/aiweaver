# State 1 - Delivery Design

### **Goal**

Design how delivery will operate in practice, translating validated inputs into an executable delivery system.

---

## **State Owner**

Project / Product Lead

---

## **Required Functions**

- Project / Product Lead
- Staffing / Capacity Manager
- Head of Operations

---

**Entry conditions**

- State 0 marked as **Ready to Start**

**Exit conditions (explicit and binary)**

- Global backlog created from the product roadmap
- Delivery execution rules defined
- Staffing process authorized based on Delivery Team
- State marked as **Delivery Designed**

---

### **Actions**

**Action 1 – Build Global Backlog**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: translate the product roadmap, documentation, and design into a structured and prioritized backlog suitable for continuous delivery.

Must be true after: backlog covers the full roadmap and is exploitable by the delivery team.

---

**Action 2 – Define Delivery Execution Rules**

R: Project / Product Lead · A: Project / Product Lead

Purpose: define how work is executed (kanban structure, task intake rules, quality expectations).

Must be true after: delivery execution rules are explicit, shared, and enforceable.

---

**Action 3 – Authorize Staffing Process**

R: Staffing / Capacity Manager · A: Head of Operations

Purpose: authorize recruitment or allocation based on the Delivery Team definition.

Must be true after: staffing process triggered using roles, technologies, and methodologies defined in the Delivery Team.

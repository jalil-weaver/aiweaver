# Functions - Governors

### **Head of Operations**

Owner of the operational system.

Ensures workflow compliance, arbitrates blockers, and drives overall performance.

---

### **Delivery Owner**

Ultimately accountable for client delivery.

Validates state entry/exit and escalates risks.

---

### **Project / Product Lead**

Leads day-to-day project execution.

Coordinates teams and ensures operational progress.

---

### **Tech Lead**

Makes key technical decisions.

Ensures architecture quality and technical standards.

---

### **Staffing / Capacity Manager**

Manages team capacity and workload.

Anticipates hiring, replacements, and scaling needs.

---

### **Quality & Process Guardian**

Ensures processes are properly applied.

Identifies deviations, SPOFs, and improvement opportunities.

---

### **AI Product Owner (Internal)**

Owns AI behavior and product rules.

Defines use cases, constraints, and acceptance criteria.

---

### **AI Governance / AI Tech Owner (Internal)**

Owns AI risk, security, and technical guardrails.

Ensures compliance, reversibility, and no vendor lock-in.

---

### **Client Interface (Delivery Side)**

Primary client contact during delivery.

Secures communication without sales commitments.
